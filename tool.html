Peak Periods

Mark CNY, Raya, Deepavali, Christmas, or any other busy stretch so it's highlighted on the calendar above. These shift every year (several are lunar-calendar holidays), so there's no automatic list - enter the dates yourself once a year.

Calendar Subscription (iCal)

Read-only feed of confirmed (deposit-paid/completed) events - date and event type only, no customer phone, pricing, or notes. Subscribe to this link in Google Calendar/Outlook/your phone's calendar app to see bookings alongside everything else. This link is private to you - don't share it, it has no login of its own (that's how calendar subscriptions work).

Public Services

Shown on your website's Services page. No prices here - customers never see prices.

Internal Pricing Items

Your real prices - only used inside quotations, never shown to customers. Cost (BAU backlog #37) is what this actually costs you to deliver - optional, used for margin tracking in Analytics.

Package Templates

Bundles you can quick-fill into a quotation (e.g. "Wedding Package").

Task Templates

Checklists you can apply to a booking's Tasks panel (e.g. "Wedding Checklist") - same shape as Package Templates above. Applying one is a manual action from the Tasks panel on a quotation, not automatic.

Physical Props / Inventory

Finite physical decoration items (BAU backlog #38) - e.g. a signature backdrop or arch. Tracked separately from services/pricing since a physical item, unlike a service, can only be in one place at a time. Assign these to a specific booking from the Quotations tab.

Gallery

Photos shown on your website's Gallery page.

Select multiple files to add them all at once, using the title/category/active settings below for every photo. Select just one to preview and edit its title before adding.

FAQ

Shown on your website's FAQ page.

WhatsApp Message Templates

Edit the wording used when sending a quotation or event reminder via WhatsApp. Placeholders like {{customer_name}} are filled in automatically - available tokens: {{customer_name}}, {{event_type}}, {{event_date}}, {{guest_count}}, {{quotation_no}}, {{items}}, {{total}}, {{deposit}}, {{balance}}, {{valid_until}} (not every token applies to every template).
