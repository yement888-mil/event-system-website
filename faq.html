Frequently Asked Questions

How much deposit is required? +
30% of the total quotation. The remaining balance can be paid on the event day.
Can I change the event date after booking? +
Yes, subject to availability. The first date change is complimentary. Please contact us via WhatsApp as soon as possible.
How long does decoration setup take? +
Setup typically takes 2–3 hours before the event begins. Our team arrives early to ensure everything is ready on time.
Can I choose a decoration theme? +
Yes! We offer more than 20 decoration themes. Our theme catalogue will be shared after your booking deposit is confirmed.
How many photos do we receive? +
You'll receive more than 200 professionally edited photos via Google Drive within one week after the event.
What age is the magic show suitable for? +
The magic show is recommended for children aged 4–12 years. It lasts approximately 45 minutes and is highly interactive.
What's included in the catering package? +
A full catering menu will be shared after your booking is confirmed.
Can I add services after booking? +
Yes, subject to availability. Any additional charges will be communicated before confirmation.
What's the cancellation policy? +
Deposits are non-refundable for cancellations made less than 7 days before the event.
How long can the event run? +
Our standard event duration is 4 hours. Additional hours can be arranged for an extra charge.
Which areas do you cover? +
We provide our services throughout Klang Valley, Selangor, and Kuala Lumpur.
Is there a minimum guest requirement for catering? +
Yes. Our catering service requires a minimum of 30 guests. The same pricing applies for bookings below 30 guests.
Can I view previous setups before booking? +
Yes. We can share photos or videos of our recent event setups. Physical viewing is subject to ongoing event schedules.
Can I use external vendors? +
Yes. However, we are not responsible for the products or services provided by external vendors.
How do I make a booking? +
Complete the inquiry form on our website or contact us via WhatsApp. We'll send you a quotation, and your booking is confirmed once the deposit is received.
How quickly will I receive a quotation? +
We aim to provide all quotations within 24 hours of receiving your inquiry.
Can I request a specific theme or song? +
Absolutely. You're welcome to request any theme or song, and we'll customize the event to match your preferences.
